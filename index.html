Puffy Pals AR turns your real space into a playful bubble slingshot game. Look around, find floating pals, aim with the slingshot, and pop them with soft bubble shots. It is built for quick, cheerful iPhone and iPad play.

How it plays

Look around your space

The pals appear in your real environment, so each round feels a little different depending on where you play.

Follow the indicator

Direction hints help you find pals when they drift outside the camera view, keeping the game moving.

Aim the slingshot

Pull back, watch the aiming arc, and line up a soft bubble shot before launching.

Pop and keep going

Clear playful stages, try different shot styles, and keep chasing cute bubble-pop moments.
