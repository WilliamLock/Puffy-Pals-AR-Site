Privacy Policy for Puffy Pals AR

Effective date: June 12, 2026

Puffy Pals AR is an augmented reality bubble slingshot game for iPhone and iPad. The app is designed for local play and does not require an account.

Information We Collect

Puffy Pals AR does not collect personal information. The app does not include advertising, analytics SDKs, account sign-in, subscriptions, in-app purchases, chat, user-generated content, or unrestricted web browsing.

Camera and AR

The app uses the device camera and ARKit to show gameplay in your real-world environment. Camera frames and AR tracking information are used on device to run the game and are not sent to the developer.

Game Progress

Any local gameplay state or settings are used only to run the game on your device.

Children

Puffy Pals AR is intended as a family-friendly casual game. It does not include social features, gambling, or user-generated content.

Changes

If the app's privacy practices change, this policy and the App Store privacy information will be updated.

Contact

For privacy questions or support, visit Support.
